Our Mission

At Tastes of Africa, our mission is to promote and celebrate the diverse and rich culinary traditions of Africa, sharing delicious recipes and the stories behind them with the world.

Founded by Onyx, this archive documents the living food cultures of Rwanda, South Africa, Somalia, and Ghana — four nations whose cuisines deserve to be known, cooked, and celebrated everywhere.

History of African Cuisine

African cuisine is a tapestry of flavors, reflecting the continent's diverse cultures and history. From the spiced stews of North Africa to the hearty dishes of Southern Africa, African food has influenced and been influenced by global culinary traditions over centuries.

Many of the world's staple foods trace their origins to Africa. Coffee was first discovered in Ethiopia. Sorghum, millet, and teff were cultivated in sub-Saharan Africa thousands of years before other grains dominated global agriculture. Through the transatlantic slave trade, African culinary knowledge transformed food cultures across the Americas.

What We Offer

On Tastes of Africa, you'll find a collection of authentic African recipes categorized by region — North Africa, West Africa, East Africa, and Southern Africa — allowing you to explore the unique flavors of each area. We offer step-by-step recipes, cooking tips, cultural insights, and nutritional information to help you understand and appreciate the diversity of African cuisine.

Every recipe is presented with a video, an origin map, detailed instructions, and a curated ingredients list. Each dish tells a story — and Onyx is here to make sure those stories are heard.

Onyx — The Curator

Onyx is a culinary archivist dedicated to preserving and sharing the depth of African food heritage. Through careful research, community stories, and authentic recipes, Onyx brings the tastes of the continent to food lovers worldwide.
